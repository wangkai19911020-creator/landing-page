About Us

Dedicated to helping you make smarter purchasing decisions through rigorous research, testing, and expert analysis.

Our Mission

At Consumer Verification, we believe that everyone deserves to sleep better and live healthier. The marketplace is flooded with products making bold claims, and it can be difficult to separate marketing hype from genuine quality.

Our mission is simple: to provide honest, comprehensive, and data-driven reviews of health and wellness products. We spend hours researching, testing, and analyzing products so you don't have to.

How We Rate Products

Our rating system is built on a foundation of objectivity and transparency. We don't just look at the price tag; we dig deeper into what makes a product truly valuable.

1. Research & Analysis

We start by analyzing market trends, scientific studies, and verified customer feedback to identify the top contenders in each category.

2. Evaluation Criteria

For grounding sheets and wellness products, we evaluate based on specific criteria: Conductivity, Material Quality, Durability, Comfort, and Value for Money.

3. The Final Score

We combine our findings to assign a final score to each product, helping us rank them from top to bottom based on overall performance.
